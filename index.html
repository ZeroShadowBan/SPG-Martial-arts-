About Us

SPG Martial Arts offers top-level Brazilian jiu-jitsu classes led by Shane Price, a 1st Degree Blackbelt. Shane is a two-time European Nogi Champion and a Brown Belt World Silver Medalist. He also holds Blackbelts in Judo and Kickboxing, and has competed worldwide in these martial arts, in addition to winning an MMA title.

Alongside Shane, we have a dedicated team of coaches including Andrew Killen for kids classes, Rob Rutter for fundamentals, and Callum Foster for a Saturday class. All of our instructors bring years of experience to ensure a well-rounded and supportive training environment for all skill levels.

We are based in the Warehouse Gym Swansea, a UK award-winning facility where members enjoy full access to both martial arts and gym equipment.
